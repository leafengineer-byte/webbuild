🌍 Our Mission

Herbal Garden is dedicated to educating people about the power of medicinal plants. We combine ancient wisdom with modern technology through Augmented Reality to create an immersive learning experience.

💡 What We Offer

  • Comprehensive **Herbal Library** with medicinal benefits.
  • **AR Scanner** to identify plants in real time.
  • **Virtual Garden** for interactive plant exploration.
  • **Community & Forum** for knowledge sharing.

👨‍💻 Meet the Team

We are a group of nature enthusiasts, herbalists, and tech experts passionate about bridging the gap between traditional medicine and cutting-edge technology.
